Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

PromptLabs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All user data is stored locally in your browser's session storage.

Prompt Data: The prompts you create are processed in real-time and are not stored by us. They are sent to the Groq API for processing and the response is displayed to you.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing our services:

- To generate and enhance your AI prompts
- To provide the "Act As" feature functionality
- To improve user experience on our platform

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the Groq API for AI processing. When you use our prompt generation features, your input is sent to Groq's servers for processing. We encourage you to review Groq's privacy policy at groq.com/privacy-policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Since we do not store your data on our servers, your data remains under your control. You can clear your browser's session storage at any time to remove all locally stored data.
